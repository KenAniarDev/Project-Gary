How it works

VibraGoons Is a first edition completely unique NFT collectible trading game that will diverse itself around multiple blockchains. Create sets of legendary Goons and win prizes as you navigate for Goons on different chains.

What makes VibraGoons different from other NFT collectibles? Passive rewards for collection holders - inspired by safemoon we have built in a fee so that every time someone sends or sells a goon they will need and extra 100 trx to make the trasnfer. This goes in to a pool and distributed back to the collection holders. All Goons are 100% unique. They are stitched together by an algorithm, but all attributes are hand made from Microsoft paint. Don’t just collect your goons to sell or show off. Collect them to win prizes in future NFT based games. Maybe even use them in future 2d animated game
